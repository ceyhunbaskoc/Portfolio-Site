🎮 About the Game
Space Crew Survival is a solo project I developed in 48 hours for Cave Jam 25! 🚀
In this game, you control a 4-person crew and your goal is to manage their stress levels. The higher the stress, the lower the oxygen! Players must prevent oxygen from dropping below 10% while completing tasks and keeping the crew alive.

You can go to this link to play the game : Mağara Jam Page

Randomly spawned 9 different objects can either increase or decrease crew stress. Players need to experiment and discover which objects have positive or negative effects, forming the core strategic mechanic of the game.

🌠 Meteor Mini-Game
Outside the main spaceship area, a mini-game occasionally triggers. Meteors fall towards the ship, and players must dodge them for 10 seconds. Colliding with meteors increases crew stress, adding reflex-based challenges to the core stress management gameplay.

💡 Features

  • Crew Management: Control stress levels of 4 crew members to keep oxygen above 10%.
  • Stress & Oxygen Mechanics: Interact with 9 different objects to strategically manage stress.
  • Meteor Dodge Mini-Game: Reflex and strategy combined; survive meteor showers for 10 seconds.
  • Random Spawning: Objects and meteors spawn at random positions and speeds, ensuring every playthrough is unique.
  • Solo Development: Despite starting 24 hours late, I completed this game alone in 48 hours.

🛠️ My Contributions

  • UI/UX: Designed visual indicators for stress levels and oxygen status for intuitive crew management.
  • Procedural Spawning: Randomized object and meteor spawns with varying positions and speeds.

🚀 How to Play

  • Objective: Keep the crew’s oxygen above 10% while managing stress and surviving meteor mini-games.
  • Controls:
    • Arrow Keys / WASD: Move the crew / navigate the spaceship in the meteor mini-game.
  • Tips:
    • Pay attention to spawned objects: Some increase stress, some reduce it.
    • Avoid meteor collisions in mini-game to prevent stress spikes.
    • High stress rapidly depletes oxygen—manage carefully!
