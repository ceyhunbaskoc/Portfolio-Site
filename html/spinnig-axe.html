🪓 Spinning Axe: Timber Defense

[Unity | License: MIT]
Genre: Idle Defense / Action Strategy
Status: In Development

🎮 About the Game

Spinning Axe: Timber Defense is a pixel-style idle defense and progression game where you defend your forest using a single enchanted axe.

  • Your weapon spins, slices, and smashes through enemies and trees automatically.
  • With each upgrade, it becomes deadlier, smarter, and more powerful.
  • You don’t directly control attacks — your axe detects nearby enemies or trees and attacks them automatically.
  • Every few hits, the axe manifests physically for a short burst, dealing massive damage to everything around it.
  • Goal: survive, upgrade, and build the ultimate forest defense — your own castle — piece by piece.

🌟 Key Features

🪓 Intelligent Auto-Attack System

  • Automatically targets nearby enemies and trees.
  • Focus on building and upgrading while the axe spins through foes.

💥 Active Power Burst

  • Every 8 hits, the axe materializes on-screen for 5 seconds.
  • Becomes a visual and mechanical powerhouse of destruction.

🧩 14 Unique Upgrades

  • Unlock upgrades that alter axe behavior or improve defenses.
  • Choose between:
    • Attack boosts
    • Faster rotations
    • Larger detection range
    • Unique passive bonuses

🏰 Castle Upgrade System

  • Earn wood at the end of each run — used to improve your castle in the main menu.
  • Castle upgrades go up to Level 5.
  • Each level adds one archer tower per corner.

🏹 Archer Tower Integration

  • Castle and towers automatically shoot arrows at enemies.
  • Strengthens the automatic defense system.

🌲 Resource-Based Progression

  • Gather wood from fallen trees and defeated enemies.
  • Spend wood to upgrade the castle, enhance upgrades, and unlock new tiers of strength.

🎇 Particle-Rich Visuals & Feedback

  • Dynamic particles, screen shakes, and smooth UI animations for every attack and upgrade.

🔄 Core Gameplay Loop

  1. Defend the Forest: Axe attacks enemies and trees automatically.
  2. Choose Upgrades: After each wave, select from 3 random upgrades out of 14.
  3. Earn and Collect Wood: Survive to gather enough currency.
  4. Return to the Castle: Spend wood to upgrade the castle.
  5. Level Up the Castle: Each level adds new archer towers.
  6. Repeat: Start stronger, go further, build your perfect defense.

🛠️ Technical Highlights

  • Built entirely in Unity (C#) with a custom object pooling system.
  • Full auto-targeting AI for axe behavior.
  • Modular UpgradeManager system handles rarity, pool selection, and UI linking.
  • Optimized for mobile and desktop.
  • Pause-safe unscaled particles ensure effects play correctly even when paused.

🧱 The Castle System

  • Castle is your permanent progress hub.
  • Level 1: Basic wooden base
  • Level 2: Adds first corner archer tower
  • Level 3: Adds second tower and visual improvements
  • Level 4: Third tower and sturdier structure
  • Level 5: Full fortress with four archer towers, all firing arrows

Each castle upgrade strengthens both visuals and automatic defenses.
